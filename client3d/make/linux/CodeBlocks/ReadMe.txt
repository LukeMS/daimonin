//===================================================
// Daimonin Client3d as Code::Blocks project HOWTO
//===================================================

1) For sound support you need the fmod library.
   Look on their HP for all information.
   (as root) copy client3d/make/linux/libfmod*.so to /usr/lib
2) Edit the plugins.cfg (set destination of PluginFolder)
2) open the project-file in code::blocks
3) press the rebuild button
